Medium Sized Business Services

Businesses with 20 - 50 employees generally find these services most cost effective:

  • Custom app development - Are you looking for a custom mobile app that provides your services such as loyalty cards, product catalogues, proprietary information, etc. to your customers? We have the experience designing, developing, and launching iPhone and Android applications to ensure your mobile project is done right.
     
  • iPhone training - Are your executives, partners, or customers lost when it comes to their iPhone? You can be seen as a thought leader by helping them learn how to get the most out of their iPhone.
     
  • Wireframes or Mockups - Does your team have the programming skills - Objective-C and Java necessary to complete a mobile app, but are lacking the design experience? We have experience with what works well for mobile applications and what does not. Let us help your project start off designed correctly so you don't risk delivering a poorly designed app that frustrates users or even lowers your revenue.
     
  • Solution Recommendation - Do you know you want to be in mobile but are struggling with what the best first project is? Let us help analyze your strengths, opportunities, and weaknesses to help you capitalize on the best opportunity.
     
  • UX Report - Have you completed a mobile app and are wondering if it works well? We can help by testing your app on focus groups. We can deliver a report along with video of actual customer usage.
     
  • Business Case Development - Wondering if it makes sense to go forward with a mobile application? Let us help you  develop a business case for the project to move forward or not.
     
  • Staff Augmentation - Do you already have a development team in place and want some experience from those who have delivered iPhone and Android applications before? We can help bring an experienced resource into your team to reduce the riskof making mistakes that cost tens to hundreds of thousands of dollars later to fix.
Can one of these services help your business? Contact us so we can learn more about your business and how we can help achieve your goals.
