iPhone Training

Do you want to do more with your iPhone? In our experience many smartphone owners know they are not taking full advantage of their device, but are embarrassed or don't want to inconvenience others by asking for help. From beginner's courses to advanced courses we can show you how to get the most out of your iPhone.

Our iPhone courses are delivered by our Chief App Maker David Smith. David loves giving presentations and is certified by Toastmasters at the Advanced Communicator Bronze level.

Course Listing:

Introduction to iPhone

Learn how to use your  iPhone! Learn about gestures, the app store, settings, driving directions, photography, and more! This full day training workshop is targeted at users who recently received their iPhone. It will walk through the most useful features the iPhone offers so attendees get the most out of their smartphone.

How to get More from Your iPhone

Become a power user. This three hour training workshop is targeted at intermediate users or users who have had their iPhone for about 2 years but feel there is too much they don't know about their device. It skips the basics and shows users the broad range of capabilities apps provide and how to find apps that make your life more convenient. 

Ten iPhone Apps that will Blow Your Mind

Dazzle your friends at parties with the latest apps. This 90 minute interactive presentation is targeted at advanced iPhone users. Those who know more than all their friends and want to keep it that way. We show cream of the crop apps and apps that are using phone features in innovative ways.
